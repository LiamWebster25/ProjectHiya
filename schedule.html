Class Information:

  • Open Gym: Gym is open to the public.
  • Beginner: Learn the basic techniques!
  • Intermediate: Refine the techniques you have learnt in the Beginner course!
  • Advanced: Push your limits as an experienced martial artist!
  • Cardio: Special cardio specific class with many different activities to improve your cardio!
  • Stretching: Come for a chill session of stretching and socialization!
  • Sparring 16+: Reserved for students who are of age 16 or older and have grasped the basics!
  • Competition: Special training reserved for people who wish to compete. Please let us know if you are interested!
