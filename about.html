About Us

This gym is ran by the proud owner who wishes nothing but to help the upcoming generation defend themselves. Our vision is to bring martial arts in a simple and engaging way to people of all ages.

How did we start?

This gym started out just a man in his garage helping his friends learn how to avoid getting punched in the face, but quickly scaled into something much more beautiful; helping strangers not get punched in the face.
