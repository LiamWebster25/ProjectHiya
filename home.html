Welcome to Hiya! A place where people of all shapes and sizes can boost their confidence by learning the basics of self-defense. Do you lack confidence and feel insecure walking around late at night? Well good! That is the feeling we are here to remedy.

How did we get started?

This gym started out just a man in his garage helping his friends learn how to avoid getting punched in the face, but quickly scaled into something much more beautiful; helping strangers not get punched in the face.
